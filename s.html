Salient Features

Highly selective (around 4%) of applied candidates

Full Time fellowship of 20,000 per month for four years (revised in summer 2016)

Selection is through a national level test and a rigorous interview

Mandatory coursework

periodic review of the progress of the candidates

for students admitted in 2016, three scopus indexed publications with atleast one in a journal with an impact factor (as specified by thomson reuters) is necessary for submission of the thesis

thesis is evaluated by two examiners, one from india and one from an international university

E-learning resources accessible throughout campus
